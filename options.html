Setup:
1
Navitage to geo.ipify.org and make an account.
2
Check your email for a verification message.
3
In the top-left corner, select your username and select “my subscriptions.”
4
Copy your API KEY and enter it here:
5
You're good to go!
Instructions:
1
Simultaneously press the "w" and "a" keys on any website to gain information about the land the website occupies.
2
To learn more about the indigenous group(s) on whose land the website occupies, you may click on their names to be taken to a list of links related to that group as collected by native-land.ca.
3
For websites that you visit frequently (especially your own website), consider setting up a recurring donation to the indigenous people whose land you are using.
4
Click "return to site" to continue browsing.
Important Note:
The API only allows for 1000 free requests per month. Once that limit is exceeded, this extension will stop working until your account replenishes.
